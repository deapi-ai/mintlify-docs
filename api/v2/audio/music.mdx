---
title: "Text-to-Music"
openapi: "/openapi-v2.json POST /api/v2/audio/music"
---

Generate music tracks from text with control over lyrics, tempo, key, and style. Returns a `request_id` for status polling.

<Note>
**Prerequisite:** Consult the [Model Selection](/api/v2/utilities/models) endpoint to identify a valid model `slug` and check its parameter limits.
</Note>
